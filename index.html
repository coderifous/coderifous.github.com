Dancing with Sprockets

Enjoy the benefits of managing javascript dependencies right in the code, and serving sprocketized javascript, without the hassle of configuration. If your rails app has javascript, you should be using this plugin.

  • Your javascript (e.g. application.js) lives under app/javascripts
  • Third party javascript (e.g. jquery plugins) lives under app/javascripts/vendor
  • You add require directives to your javascript, e.g. //= require <vendor/jquery.localization.js>
  • You include your javascript using the sprockets_include_tag instead of javascript_include_tag
